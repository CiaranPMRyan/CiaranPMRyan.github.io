About

Who I am

20+ years across three disciplines

I'm a product manager and creative based in Mainz, Germany. I started as a QA tester in Belfast, ditched it to tour with my band, then landed in Germany doing photo touch-ups for a startup. That was 20+ years ago.

Since then: VFX and broadcast production, 3D artist for ZDF, SWR, and Arte across 20+ documentaries, and eventually Head of Product at Burgschneider GmbH. There I've the honour of working with Games Workshop, Hasbro/D&D, Middle Earth Enterprises, and Tomb Raider.

"An eye for detail isn't just a tagline — it's the through-line of everything I've built."

So, who am I?

Someone with a childlike enthusiasm to know how it all works.
Someone who loves to get his hands dirty and be part of the process.
Someone who takes pride in seeing the final result of a job well done.

Outside of work, you'll find me deep in Blender Geometry Nodes, homebrewing something experimental, or getting lost in yet another Roman history book.
